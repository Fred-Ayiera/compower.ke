About Us

Learn more about our organization, our mission, and the values that drive our work

Welcome to Compower Society

Compower Society is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering and uplifting communities across Kenya. We believe in the power of collective action and shared responsibility to create meaningful change.

Our approach combines direct assistance with long-term support initiatives that address the root causes of poverty, inequality, and social exclusion. We work closely with local communities to identify their needs and develop sustainable solutions.

Since our inception, we have impacted thousands of lives through our various programs and initiatives, always guided by our core values of dignity, equity, shared responsibility, and compassion.

Our Guiding Principles

The foundation of everything we do at Compower Society

Our Mission

ComPower Society is driven by a mission to empower, uplift, and transform lives across Kenya—starting from the grassroots—by providing direct assistance, long-term support, and community engagement initiatives that promote dignity, equity, and shared responsibility.

Our Vision

To become a leading society in Kenya and beyond, building networks of trust and empowering individuals to be part of the solution, while fostering a culture of collective impact and servant leadership.

Our Values

  • Dignity: We believe in promoting dignity and respect for all individuals, especially vulnerable and underprivileged groups.
  • Equity: We strive to address inequalities and promote fairness in all our initiatives.
  • Shared Responsibility: We believe in collective impact and shared responsibility, working together with communities, partners, and stakeholders to achieve our goals.
  • Compassion: We are driven by compassion and a strong desire to serve humanity.

Our Core Initiatives

The programs that make a real difference in people's lives

Pad Drive

Distributing sanitary products to schoolgirls in rural and marginalized areas to promote menstrual health and education. Our goal is to ensure no girl misses school due to lack of sanitary products.

Support This Initiative

Children's Home Visits

Regular visits to children's homes and special institutions to provide emotional support, food, toiletries, and identify areas where long-term help is needed. We believe every child deserves love and care.

Support This Initiative

Seasonal Campaigns

Organizing seasonal campaigns and events to promote our mission and values. From back-to-school drives to holiday celebrations, we bring joy and support to communities throughout the year.

Material Donations

We accept various material donations that directly support our programs and beneficiaries. Your in-kind contributions are equally valuable.
